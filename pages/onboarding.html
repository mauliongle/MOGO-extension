Welcome to MOGO!

Your free email & phone finder. No login required.

1. Pin the Extension for Easy Access

Pin the extension to your Chrome toolbar. Click the extensions (puzzle piece) icon, find MOGO, and click the pin icon.

2. Visit LinkedIn or Sales Navigator

Navigate to any LinkedIn profile or Sales Navigator search page. MOGO will automatically detect profiles and let you export data.

3. Start Finding Emails & Exporting! 🎉

Click the MOGO icon on any profile to find emails, or use the export button on search pages to bulk download contacts as CSV.

Unlimited access. No credits. No login.
